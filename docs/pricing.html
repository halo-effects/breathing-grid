Multi-Coin Trading
Higher tiers unlock more simultaneous coins: Starter trades 1, Trader trades up to 2, Pro up to 3, Elite up to 5, and Whale up to 8. The bot's two-tier scanner ranks 275+ coins every 4 hours and automatically selects the top performers. Capital is split proportionally based on each coin's scanner score ‚Äî a coin that scores twice as high gets roughly twice the allocation. Each coin runs its own independent long/short deal pair with full regime detection and adaptive parameters.
Three reasons. First, diminishing diversification ‚Äî crypto pairs are highly correlated, especially during regime shifts. Beyond 8 coins, you're adding correlation, not diversification. Second, capital depth matters ‚Äî each coin needs enough capital to run a full safety order grid on both long and short sides. Spreading $100K across 8 coins gives ~$12.5K each, which supports deep grids even at higher leverage. Add more coins and the grids get too shallow to be effective. Third, execution quality ‚Äî more coins means more API calls per cycle, which can slow the trading loop and delay fills. Eight coins is the sweet spot where you get broad market coverage without sacrificing execution speed or grid depth.
